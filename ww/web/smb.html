SMB Launcher

When you press the 'Launch SMB server' button, the Bunny's LED will either turn solid cyan or flash red.

If it is successful, it will start flashing cyan, and Windows Explorer will open to the SMB server.

If the Bunny flashes red it means you don't have Impacket installed.


WHEN YOU START THE SMB SERVER, DON'T TOUCH ANYTHING UNTIL IT STARTS FLASHING BLUE/CYAN!
