Payload Launcher

Launching a payload causes WabbitWeb to stop until the payload finishes (indicated by a slow-flashing blue LED on the Bunny).

Be aware that some payloads may force WabbitWeb to shut down, meaning after the payload is finished you have to reboot the Bunny to come back to this point.
